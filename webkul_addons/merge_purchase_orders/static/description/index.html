Merge Purchase Order

Odoo Merge Purchase Orders Module helps to reduce steps in your procurement process so you can easily manage, monitor and track your purchase orders from a vendor. You can choose to join different Purchase Quotations from the same vendor into a new quotation while choosing to cancel or delete the individual Purchase Quotations. Alternatively, you can merge other quotations into an existing quotation.

Features:

  • Join two or more Purchase quotations from the same vendor into a new single quotation.
  • The individual quotations can be selected to be cancelled or deleted after merging.
  • The admin can also merge two or more quotations into one existing quotation.
