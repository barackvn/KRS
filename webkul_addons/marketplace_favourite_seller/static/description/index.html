Odoo Marketplace Favourite Seller

Odoo Marketplace Favourite Seller is a module by which buyer/customers can add the seller to their "Favorite Sellers" list. A buyer can view the latest added products of their favorite seller. A seller can view their followers and send emails to the followers notifying about the lasted added product, etc.

User Guide

Highlighted Features

  • A customer can add their favorite Seller from seller product page.
  • A customer can add their favorite Seller from Seller Profile page.
  • A customer can see the number of followers of the sellers.
  • A customer can receive a notification message from the seller.
  • A customer can see all the updates and the latest product added by the seller.
  • A customer can also delete their favorite seller.
  • A seller can see and delete their followers.
  • A seller can send Emails to all their followers at ones.
  • A seller can edit an email send to any follower.
  • Admin can configure whether to send mail to the customer on a new product addition of their Favourite Seller.

Website View of Odoo Marketplace Favourite Seller

"Add as Favourite Seller" link will be visible on Seller's Product Page.

The user can click on the link to add the seller as Favourite.

"Add as Favourite Seller" link will also be visible on Seller's Profile Page.

A user can click on the link to add this seller as Favourite.

My Favourite Seller menu will be visible under My Account section of the user.

A user can view the list of his Favourite Sellers and can also remove them from this list.

Seller View of Odoo Marketplace Favourite Seller

My Followers menu will be visible to the seller under seller dashboard menu.

Seller can view the details and can also remove the follower.

A seller can write and send emails to their followers.

A seller can send emails to multiple followers at ones.

A seller can view the total number of followers from his profile and can directly visit there.

Admin View of Odoo Marketplace Favourite Seller

Seller Followers menu will be visible to admin under the seller dashboard menu.

Admin can also send emails to multiple followers at ones.

Admin can write and send the emails to the followers of marketplace's sellers.

Admin can also view the total number of followers from the seller profile.

Configuration View of Odoo Marketplace Favourite Seller

Admin can configure whether to send an email to followers of a seller when a

new product is published by the seller.
