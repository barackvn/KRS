Website Stock Notify

Website stock notify module is send e-mail notification to their customers regarding the product availability

Features:

  • For out of stock products customer can receive mail notification about the product availability.
  • Emails can be sent automatically by setting cron, when the product is back in stock.
    • Admin can also send emails manually.
  • Email template can be edited or created.
  • Cron Scheduler can be revised.

Module workflow

Customer can enter their valid email address to receive mail notification

Confirmation message is displayed as you click on notify
