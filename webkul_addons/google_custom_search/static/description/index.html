Google Custom Search

Looking for something that can search for a specific product among out of hundreds of products on the website?

Use our Google Custom Search Module to helps your customers search the website and instantly get the results that fit their requirements. Our App uses Google's search algorithm but restricts the results only to your website and beam up your sales. But you already have a search bar on your website, so how is the module different! The Search engine still indexes these customs searches thus increasing your SEO ranking and reducing the page bounce rate for the website.

Features:

  • Integrates Google search engine with your website itself.
  • Lets the customers search the website through Google search directly.
  • Filters the results to include only the ones related to the website store.
  • Custom searches on the website get ranked on the SERPs thus increasing the site's SEO ranking.
